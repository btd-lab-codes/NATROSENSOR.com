Project HBV e-Aptasense Project HBV e-APTASENSE aims to develop an electrochemical device that can be used to detect the presence of HBsAg and HBeAg using blood samples. Specific aptamer can be selected from a set of libraries and manufactured through chemical synthesis.
About NATROSENSOR The NATROSENSOR aims to develop a multiplex detection device that can be used to detect the presence of various antibiotics for monitoring the success of wastewater treatment facilities. it can also be used to detect the presence of various antibiotics in other substrates such as surface and ground water, and food products such as milk.
