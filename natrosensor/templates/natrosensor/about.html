About the Project

The project NATROSENSOR aims to develop a multiplex detection device that can be used to detect the presence of various antibiotics for monitoring the success of wastewater treatment facilities. it can also be used to detect the presence of various antibiotics in other substrates such as surface and ground water, and food products such as milk.
